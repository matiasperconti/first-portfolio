About Me

Hello! My name is Matias and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet. My interest in web development started back in 2020 when, due to pandemic, I decided to change my career. Looking for new oportunities I found a very interesting world in web development.

The last year I decided to study more in depth and I enrolled in a FullStack bootcamp, where I had a more formal education. The experience was great, by the end of the course I was able to develop my first pages and have a good understanding of various technologies. Then I realized that UX/UI design was the part where I want to focus, so I enrolled in various courses.

Currently I'm studying on new courses and on my own, developing new projects that I found interesting with inspiration I found across internet. Here are a few technologies I’ve been working with recently:

  • Figma
  • Adobe Creative Suite
  • Javascript
  • SASS
  • Node.JS
  • Git
